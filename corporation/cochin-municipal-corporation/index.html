Milestone in the Evolution of Kochi Municipal Corporation

1866 - Fort Kochi becomes a Municipality.

1986 - Maharaja of Kochi formed sanitary boards for Mattanchery and Ernakulam

Town councils of Mattanchery and Ernakulam formed.

The provisions of Madras Town Planning Act 1920 were applicable to Fort Kochi and the Travancore Town Planning Regulations 1108 (M.E). ie 1932 were made applicable to Mattanchery and Ernakulam1952 - Edappally bifurcated - Vennala.

1956 - the Elamkulam Panchayat and a portion of Cheranellur Panchayat were amalgamated to the Ernakulam Municipality.

1962 - a portion of Pallurathy Panchayat (Mundamveli area) was amalgamated to the then Mattanchery Municipality1st July 1967, Kerala Assembly approved the formation of Kochi Municipaity.

Nov 1967 - Kochi Municipal Corporation was formed
